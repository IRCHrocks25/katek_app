Data Deletion

Request data deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated operational data at any time. Below is how the process works and how to contact us.

Overview

Katek respects your right to control your data. If you would like your account and the operational data associated with it removed from our platform, you can request deletion by emailing our team. Requests are reviewed and processed in line with platform policies, applicable law, and any operational requirements that apply to your account.

Step 01

Send a request

Email support@katek.app from the email address associated with your Katek account.

Step 02

Verification

We’ll verify the request to ensure it’s coming from the rightful account holder and confirm what should be deleted.

Step 03

Processing

Once verified, we’ll process the deletion in line with platform policies and confirm completion via email.

What gets deleted

  • Your Katek account and authentication information
  • Profile information you submitted to the platform
  • Operational data associated with your account, such as workflows, configurations, and stored content
  • Session tokens and identifiers tied to your account

What we may retain

We may retain limited information when required by law, for security and fraud prevention, or to enforce our agreements. Where possible, retained data is minimized and access is restricted.

Suggested email template

To make your request faster to process, you can use the template below.

To: support@katek.app Subject: Data Deletion Request Hello Katek team, I’m writing to request deletion of my account and associated operational data. Account email: [your email here] Organization (if applicable): [your organization] Please confirm once the deletion has been processed. Thank you, [Your name]

Processing time

We aim to process verified deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe, in line with platform policies and operational requirements. We’ll let you know if we need any additional information or if your request will take longer to complete.

Questions

If you have questions about this process — including what data we hold or how deletion is performed — contact us at support@katek.app or visit our Privacy Policy.

Ready to request deletion?

Send an email and our team will guide you through the process from there.

The Problem

Too many tools. Not enough coordination.

Most organizations run on disconnected software — a CRM here, a chat tool there, an automation app somewhere else. The gaps between them are where work disappears, visibility breaks down, and operational efficiency stalls.

Workflows trapped in inboxes

When operational processes live inside individual email threads, coordination breaks the moment someone is unavailable.

No visibility across teams

Without centralized coordination, teams duplicate work, miss handoffs, and operate with outdated information. Status becomes a full-time job.

Systems that don't talk to each other

Disconnected software creates manual bridging work. Data doesn't flow — it gets copied, re-entered, and eventually lost.

Knowledge locked in people, not systems

When operational knowledge exists only in people's heads, onboarding slows, errors multiply, and scaling becomes unpredictable.

How It Works

Built as connected operational layers.

The Katek ecosystem is structured across three distinct operational layers — each handling a different function, all working together to give organizations greater consistency, visibility, and coordination.

Layer 01 — Intelligence

AI systems and operational intelligence accessed through AI KONIK. This layer handles structured human-AI collaboration, intelligent task assistance, and the decision-support infrastructure that powers execution across the platform.

Layer 02 — Coordination

Workflow orchestration and operational routing managed through Orchestra. Approvals, sequencing, conditional logic, escalations, and process management — all handled systematically so work moves forward without manual intervention at every step.

Layer 03 — Application

Business-facing systems including AI Business OS, SOP Master, and CourseForge. These are the tools your teams use every day — built on the intelligence and coordination infrastructure below, so they work together rather than in isolation.

One login. One workspace. All systems connected.

Katek provides centralized platform access through a unified authentication environment. Users access the specific systems relevant to their role, team, and operational responsibilities — without managing separate tools or credentials.

FAQ

Common questions about the platform.

What is Katek and what does the platform do?
Katek is an operational software platform that provides organizations with a unified environment for AI-assisted workflows, business coordination, internal training, and knowledge management. Instead of managing separate tools for each function, teams access structured systems through a single authenticated workspace.
What is the relationship between Katek and Katek AI?
Katek AI is the parent company — an AI engineering and systems firm that designs and builds operational AI infrastructure. Katek (katek.app) is the software platform ecosystem — the operational tools, workflow systems, and business applications that organizations access as a managed platform. Katek AI builds the systems; Katek is where teams use them.
How does authentication and user access work?
Katek uses centralized authentication with support for Google OAuth, email login, and enterprise SSO. Role-based access controls allow administrators to manage which systems and data each user can access. Sessions are encrypted and access is logged for security and compliance purposes.
What data does the platform collect and how is it used?
Katek collects operational data necessary to provide platform functionality — including authentication credentials, workflow data, user activity logs, and integration-related information. Data is used to deliver platform features, ensure security, and support operational continuity. We do not sell user data to third parties. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
What integrations does the platform support?
The Katek platform integrates with Gmail, Google Calendar, Outlook, major CRM systems including HubSpot and HighLevel, project management tools, communication platforms, and additional business software via API. Integrations are configured at the organization level and managed through the platform's centralized settings.
How do I request deletion of my account or data?
Users may request deletion of their account and associated operational data by contacting support@katek.app. Deletion requests are processed according to platform policies and applicable data protection requirements. Requests are typically completed within 30 days. A dedicated data deletion page is also available at katek.app/data-deletion.
